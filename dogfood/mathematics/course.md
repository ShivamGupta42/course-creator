# Mathematics, from the ground up

The promise: start from how numbers are written and end able to read a derivative,
a short proof, and a probability as ideas you can explain and check, not symbols you
memorized. Guiding question for the whole course: *what is this rule really doing,
and how would I check it on the smallest honest case?*

Each module links the shared concept(s) it teaches in
[knowledge/](knowledge/). The teaching stays unique per module; the concept files
are the reusable, cited layer.

## Track 1: Foundations of number and structure (9 modules)

- Module 01: How numbers are written: place value and the line. Teaches [place value and the number line](knowledge/place-value.md).
- Module 02: Combining numbers: the four operations and their order. Teaches [operations and order of operations](knowledge/operations-and-order.md).
- Module 03: Fractions and ratios as division you can see. Teaches [place value and the number line](knowledge/place-value.md), [operations and order of operations](knowledge/operations-and-order.md).
- Module 04: Letters for numbers: variables and expressions. Teaches [variables and expressions](knowledge/variables-and-expressions.md).
- Module 05: Solving a linear equation by balancing. Teaches [linear equations](knowledge/linear-equations.md).
- Module 06: Negative numbers and the meaning of subtraction. Teaches [operations and order of operations](knowledge/operations-and-order.md).
- Module 07: Powers, roots, and what an exponent counts. Teaches [operations and order of operations](knowledge/operations-and-order.md).
- Module 08: Rearranging expressions without changing their value. Teaches [variables and expressions](knowledge/variables-and-expressions.md).
- Module 09: Word problems: turning a sentence into an equation. Teaches [linear equations](knowledge/linear-equations.md), [variables and expressions](knowledge/variables-and-expressions.md).

## Track 2: Functions, change, and reasoning (9 modules)

- Module 10: What a function is: one input, one output. Teaches [functions](knowledge/functions.md).
- Module 11: Reading and drawing graphs. Teaches [functions](knowledge/functions.md).
- Module 12: Slope: how fast the output moves. Teaches [slope and rate of change](knowledge/slope-and-rate.md).
- Module 13: Lines, intercepts, and the equation y = mx + c. Teaches [slope and rate of change](knowledge/slope-and-rate.md), [linear equations](knowledge/linear-equations.md).
- Module 14: What counts as a proof: logic from the ground up. Teaches [logic and proof](knowledge/logic-and-proof.md).
- Module 15: Quadratics and the shape of a parabola. Teaches [functions](knowledge/functions.md).
- Module 16: Exponential growth and why it outruns lines. Teaches [functions](knowledge/functions.md).
- Module 17: Limits: where a function is heading. Teaches [limits](knowledge/limits.md).
- Module 18: The derivative: the slope at an instant. Teaches [the derivative](knowledge/derivative.md).

## Track 3: Calculus intuition, logic, and chance (7 modules)

- Module 19: Reading a derivative: increasing, flat, and turning. Teaches [the derivative](knowledge/derivative.md).
- Module 20: Proof techniques: direct, contradiction, induction. Teaches [logic and proof](knowledge/logic-and-proof.md).
- Module 21: Sets and counting without listing every case. Teaches [sets and counting](knowledge/sets-and-counting.md).
- Module 22: Probability: measuring how often things happen. Teaches [probability](knowledge/probability.md).
- Module 23: Expectation: the long-run average. Teaches [expectation](knowledge/expectation.md).
- Module 24: Combining events: independence and conditional probability. Teaches [probability](knowledge/probability.md), [sets and counting](knowledge/sets-and-counting.md).
- Module 25: Putting it together: a rate, a proof, and a fair bet. Teaches [the derivative](knowledge/derivative.md), [logic and proof](knowledge/logic-and-proof.md), [expectation](knowledge/expectation.md).
